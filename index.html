About Me

I am an Assistant Professor of Information Systems and Business Analytics at the Neil Griffin College of Business, Arkansas State University. My work focuses on the intersection of technology, business analytics, and operations management.

My research explores the operational challenges and opportunities presented by disruptive technologies such as autonomous vehicles and blockchain. I am particularly interested in applying Machine Learning (especially Deep Reinforcement Learning), Game Theory, and Optimization to solve complex problems in supply chain management and insurance operations.
